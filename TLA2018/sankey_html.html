Project: Similarities Between George Whitfield's Sermons
Description: Measure the similarities between 57 of George Whitfield's sermons to eachother
Source: CRTA (Center for Reformed Theology and Apologetics)
Calculation Method: TF-IDF Cosine Difference (code) (tabular results)
Visualization Method: Sankey Chart using Google Charts JavaScript Classes. All similarity scores .35 or higher (35% similar) displayed.
